What we offer

    Plastic recycling

    In our recycling centers, you can hand over plastic and recyclables, and we will recycle it into something useful and will not pollute the ocean. And to the one who gives up more than 100 kilograms, we will give Vitaly the octopus a pat)

    Diving

    We will be happy to take you on an underwater walk through the picturesque coral reefs, the habitat of many marine inhabitants.

    Oceanarium tours and curatorship

    Excursions to our oceanariums are always available for you, where you can get to know marine life better and even become a curator of one of them.
